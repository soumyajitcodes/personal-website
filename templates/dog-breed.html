Dog Breed Identification

This project predicts the breed of a dog given as image to it. As the data is unstructured data, so I have used Deep Learning and Transfer Learning.
Deep Learning does not refer to any one specific algorithm, but rather a general “philosophy of machine learning” that uses very big neural networks, often trained on huge amounts of data.
Transfer Learning as a field has existed before DL, and attempts to solve the problem of “given a learned model that performs well on one task, modify the model so that it solves another task”.
I downloaded the data from Kaggle.
The project predicts the breed of the dog at an accuracy of over 90%.
